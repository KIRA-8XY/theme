Our Core Values

Peta Vegan & Cruelty Free

Our core value is to distribute our well-crafted natural skincare products at affordable prices, with no animal testing. We are proud to be a clean beauty brand and have acquired certifications to prove it, such as CGMP, Beauty without Bunnies, and cruelty-free & vegan through PETA.

EWG Green Grade

SKIN1004's Centella is certified as EWG green grade, imported with minimized production process, and has removed unnecessary ingredients to create safe and mild skincare products.

Artificial Fragrance Free

All SKIN1004 products are free of artificial fragrances, containing only scents from natural ingredients.

Why Choose SKIN1004

At Skin1004, we use 100% pure Centella Asiatica extract sourced from the untouched regions of Madagascar, known for its powerful skin-soothing and healing properties. Perfect for sensitive, acne-prone, and irritated skin, our products bring nature's best to your skincare routine.
